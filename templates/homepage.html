Where do we get our data?

All of the genetic variants here are from women who are older than 70 and have never had cancer. Researchers at the University of Washington and Color Genomics sequenced the DNA from 10,000 participatants in the Women's Health Initiative (WHI), a long term national health study focused on strategies for preventing heart disease, breast and colorectal cancer, and osteoporotic fractures in postmenopausal women.
