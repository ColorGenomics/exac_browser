Where do we get our data?

All of the genetic variants found in FLOS(sie) are from women who are older than 70 and have never had cancer. They participated in the Women's Health Initiative (WHI), a long term national health study focused on strategies for preventing heart disease, breast and colorectal cancer, and osteoporotic fractures in postmenopausal women.

Researchers at the University of Washington and Color Genomics sequenced the DNA from 10,000 of these "Fine Ladies Over Seventy" to create FLOS(sie).
